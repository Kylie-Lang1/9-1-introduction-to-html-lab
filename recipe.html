Delicious Deviled Eggs

Description

Deviled eggs are a great snack that can be enjoyed year-round. Dress them up with a little food coloring to add a festive touch.

Ingredients

  • 6 Eggs
  • 2 tbs Mayonnaise
  • 1 tbs Sour cream
  • 1 tsp Mustard
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • Paprika (for garnish)
  • Chives (for garnish)

Directions

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil with enough water to cover all eggs.
  2. Once boiling, gently place eggs in the water with a serving spoon.
  3. Slightly lower to medium heat and allow eggs to boil for 12 minutes.
  4. Remove eggs from water with a slotted spoon and place eggs in an ice water bath.
  5. Carefully peel eggs, ensuring the egg stays whole.
  6. Cut eggs in half and scoop out yolks into a large mixing bowl.
  7. Use a fork to mash yolks and mix in mayonnaise, sour cream, mustard, salt and pepper.
  8. Spoon yolk mixture back into halved eggs (or use a piping bag).
  9. Garnish with paprika and chives. Then enjoy!
